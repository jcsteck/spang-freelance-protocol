FAQ

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

  1. HOW SHOULD I ORGANIZE MY PROJECT? -How you organize your project will have a significant impact on the mental health of whoever opens it later. After numerous mental breakdowns, we created a  folder structure that's worked so far. Please, use it.
  2. DO I BACKUP MY PROJECT? - Yes! Please compress all your project files and upload them onto Egnyte, once you are done for the day.
  3. WHAT IF YOUR FOLDER STRUCTURE IS NOT IDEAL FOR MY KIND OF PROJECT? -There will always be exceptions to the rule. If you feel like your project needs to be structured in a different way, please contact management so we can agree on a structure that's convenient for you but also fits Spang's standard.
  4. WHAT IF I LIKE ALL MY FILES SCATTERED ON THE DESKTOP? - Then it is time for an intervention to confront your bad habit. All of your project assets must live on the hard drive assigned for that project. If, on top of that, you follow our folder structure, then, we'll love you.
  5. DO YOU HAVE AN SFX LIBRARY? - And Adobe doesn't like you, but, as of now, it is the industry standard and we need a Project that we can access and modify in case we need to after you have completed the project.

EQUIPMENT

  1. WHAT EDITING SOFTWARE DO YOU RUN? - Adobe Creative Suite and DaVinci Resolve.
  2. I'VE GOT MY OWN 100TB SSD DRIVE THAT I'D LIKE TO USE - You are free to backup to your drive; however, Spang is responsible for the project's files and workflow. Please use the harddrive we provide. However, feel free to use your drive for Cache or to keep a backup or your work.
  3. I ONLY USE PALETTE GEAR TO EDIT - No problem, feel free to bring it along and install the drivers necessary. We are not always able to provide specific equipment in house. We have purchased items such as a Premiere keyboard for editors, but we do not have tablets in-house. 
  4. IS SPANG POST OPEN TO ADVICE - Please, we are all ears! Think a plugin or piece of equipment would benefit the workflow? Let's discuss!

OFFICE

  1. IS EVERYONE COOL THERE? - We try really hard to balance our nerdy and cool sides. Please let us know if something's making you uncomfortable and we'll do our best to change it.
  2. DO YOU HAVE A MAID? -We don't. Please clean up after yourself.
  3. ARE ALL THESE SNACKS FOR ME? - Some could be, but I do work hard to provide special items for specific clients. Let me know if you'd like a certain snack or beverage and I'd be happy to pick it up for you! Neither you nor Cali are allowed to sneak snacks behind my back.
  4. WHO'S CALI? - This is Cali. She will lay under the edit desk to keep you relaxed, simply watch out for her tail.

PAYMENTS

  1. WHAT PAYMENT DETAILS WILL SPANG PROVIDE? - Spang will provide you with a P.O. for the job. This should include the job number, your designated position for the job, and payment details.
  2. WHAT WILL I OWE SPANG? - At the completion of the job Spang should have your invoice and W-9 in hand to complete your payment. Please be sure to include the job number, PO number and date on your invoice.
  3. TO WHOM DO I SEND MY INVOICE? - Please email your invoice and W-9 to maggie@spangtv.com and carbon copy invoices@spangtv.com
  4. WHEN CAN I EXPECT PAYMENT FOR MY SERVICES? - We work to get you paid within 30 days of the completion of the project. Remember we produce large jobs, but our company is small. If you have questions about your payment after 30 days, simply ask and we'll dig in.
